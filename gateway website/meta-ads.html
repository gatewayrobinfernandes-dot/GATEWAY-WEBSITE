Meta Ads FAQs

Common questions about our Meta advertising services

How long does it take to see results from Meta Ads?

Most campaigns start showing meaningful results within 2-4 weeks as the algorithm learns and optimizes. Significant ROI typically appears within 1-2 months with proper optimization and budget allocation.

What's the minimum ad spend required?

Our Starter package requires a minimum ad spend of ₹20,000 per month. For smaller budgets, we offer consultation services to help you build effective campaigns that you can manage yourself.

Do you work with e-commerce businesses?

Yes, we specialize in e-commerce Meta advertising with expertise in product catalog feeds, dynamic ads, shopping campaigns, and conversion optimization for online stores.

How do you measure campaign success?

We track multiple KPIs including ROAS (Return on Ad Spend), CPA (Cost Per Acquisition), CTR (Click-Through Rate), and conversion rates. We provide detailed monthly reports with actionable insights.
