Social Media Marketing FAQs

Common questions about our social media marketing services

How long does it take to see results from social media marketing?

Initial engagement improvements can be seen within 2-4 weeks, while significant business results (leads, sales) typically appear within 3-6 months. Consistent effort and strategy optimization are key to long-term success.

Which social media platforms should my business be on?

The ideal platforms depend on your target audience and business goals. We analyze your specific needs to recommend the right mix - Facebook for broad reach, Instagram for visual brands, LinkedIn for B2B, Twitter for real-time engagement, etc.

Do you provide content creation as part of your services?

Yes, all our packages include content creation - from basic posts in starter plans to premium custom content in enterprise packages. We create platform-optimized content that resonates with your audience.

How do you measure social media marketing success?

We track multiple KPIs including engagement rate, reach, website traffic, lead generation, conversion rate, and ROI. You'll receive detailed monthly reports with insights and recommendations for improvement.
