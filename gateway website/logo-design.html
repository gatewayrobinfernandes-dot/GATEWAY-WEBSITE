Logo Design FAQs

Common questions about our logo design services

How long does the logo design process take?

The timeline depends on the package. Basic logos take 5-7 days, Professional packages take 10-14 days, and Premium packages take 2-3 weeks. We provide detailed timelines after understanding your requirements.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all final logos in vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF, SVG) for scalability and raster formats (PNG, JPG) for web and print use. We also provide transparent background versions.

Do I own the copyright to my logo?

Yes, once the project is completed and payment is received, you own full copyright and commercial rights to your logo design.

Can you help with trademark registration?

While we're not legal experts, we can provide all necessary files and documentation for trademark registration and guide you through the process. We recommend consulting with a trademark attorney for legal protection.
