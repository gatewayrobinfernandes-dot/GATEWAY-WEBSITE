Branding FAQs

Common questions about our branding services

How long does the branding process take?

The timeline depends on the package and complexity. A basic logo design takes 1-2 weeks, while a complete brand identity system can take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during the discovery phase.

Do you provide brand strategy along with design?

Yes, we believe strong branding starts with strategy. All our packages include strategic elements, with more comprehensive strategy development in our Professional and Enterprise packages.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all final logos in vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF) and raster formats (PNG, JPG) for various applications. We also provide source files and comprehensive brand guidelines.

Can you help with trademarking our new brand?

While we're not legal experts, we can guide you through the process and provide all necessary files and documentation for trademark registration. We recommend consulting with a trademark attorney for legal protection.
