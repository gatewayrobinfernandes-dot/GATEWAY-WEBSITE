Game Development FAQs

Common questions about our game development services

How long does game development typically take?

Development time varies by project scope. Simple mobile games take 3-6 months, mid-range projects 6-12 months, and complex AAA-quality games can take 12-24 months or more. We provide detailed timelines after understanding your requirements.

What platforms can you develop games for?

We develop for iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, Linux, and web platforms. We also have experience with console development for PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch through partner programs.

Do you provide post-launch support and updates?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance optimization, content updates, and new feature development. We provide flexible support packages based on your needs.

Can you help with game publishing and marketing?

While our primary focus is development, we can provide guidance on publishing strategies and connect you with our network of publishing partners. We also offer ASO (App Store Optimization) services to improve your game's visibility.
